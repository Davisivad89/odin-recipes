Soup Recipe

Description:

Homemade chicken noodle soup recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Broth.
  • Chicken.
  • Veggies.
  • Noodles.

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken, bake or boil.
  2. Warm broth over low heat.
  3. Dice up chicken and veggies.
  4. Add noodles to broth.
  5. Add chicken and veggies to broth and noodles.
  6. Let simmer until for an hour or until ready to serve. Stirring frequently.
