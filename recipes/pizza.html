Pizza Recipe

Description:

Mostly homemade pizza! We'll be using mostly store bought items here.

Ingredients

  • Store bought dough.
  • Store bought sauce.
  • Mozzarella cheese.
  • Toppings: Meat & Veggies

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Roll out your dough.
  3. Apply sauce evenly over dough.
  4. Add light layer of cheese.
  5. Add toppings.
  6. Finish off with another layer of cheese.
  7. Allow pizza to sit for ten to fifteen minutes before serving.
